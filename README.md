📊 Sales Funnel Analysis in Power BI

🚀 Executive Summary

End-to-end sales funnel analysis project tracking customer journey from leads to contracts to identify conversion gaps and improve sales efficiency.

Tools Used: Power BI, SQL

What was done:

Built a complete sales funnel dashboard in Power BI
Analyzed stage-wise conversion rates
Evaluated salesperson performance
Identified bottlenecks in the pipeline

Key Insights:

Significant drop-off between Lead → Presentation stage
Only ~10% of total prospects convert to contracts
Top sales reps contribute majority of closed deals
Peak activity observed in specific quarters (seasonality impact)
Offers stage shows potential optimization opportunity
🧩 Business Problem

Sales team wants to:

Track performance across the sales pipeline
Identify where customers drop off
Improve conversion rates and sales efficiency
Evaluate salesperson contribution

Core Question:
How can funnel data be leveraged to optimize conversions and improve overall sales performance?

⚙️ Methodology

🛢️ Data Analysis (SQL / Data Modeling)
Structured funnel stages:
Company → Lead → Presentation → Offer → Contract
Calculated:
Conversion rates

Salesperson

📈 Results & Business Recommendations

🔍 Key Findings
High drop-off in early funnel stages (Lead → Presentation)
Only ~10% final conversion rate
Sales performance is uneven across team members
Seasonal spikes affect lead generation and conversions

📊 Metrics
Total Companies: 82
Leads: 55 (67%)
Presentations: 34 (41%)
Offers: 21 (26%)
Contracts: 8 (10%)
💡 Business Recommendations
🎯 Improve lead qualification to reduce early drop-offs
🧑‍💼 Train underperforming sales reps using top performer benchmarks
📈 Focus on high-conversion time periods for campaigns
💼 Optimize offer strategies to increase deal closure
🔁 Implement follow-up strategies between presentation and offer stages
